Oswald "Cobb" Cobblepot - The Penguin

Oswald Cobblepot, also known as the Penguin, is a complex and multifaceted character who embodies ambition, cunning, and a relentless drive for power. Initially portrayed as an outsider in Gotham's criminal underworld, Oswald rises to prominence through his intelligence, manipulation, and strategic thinking. His character is marked by a deep-seated desire to prove himself, stemming from his troubled childhood and the overbearing influence of his mother. Oswald is often depicted as both charming and ruthless, navigating the treacherous waters of Gotham's crime scene with a blend of charisma and brutality. He has a keen ability to read people and situations, which he uses to outmaneuver rivals and secure his position. Despite his criminal pursuits, Oswald's character often shows vulnerability and moments of reflection, revealing the internal conflicts between his moral compass and his ambition. His journey in the series explores themes of loyalty, betrayal, and the quest for acceptance, making him one of the most intriguing and dynamic characters in Gotham’s dark landscape.

Sofia "Falcone" Gigante - The hangman

Sofia Falcone is a formidable, intelligent, and calculating character within Gotham’s criminal underworld. As the daughter of Carmine Falcone, she brings both ambition and a ruthless streak, traits honed under her father’s legacy. Sofia is often depicted as complex—fiercely loyal to her family but also determined to establish her own influence and power. She is known for her sharp wit, manipulative skills, and willingness to take risks, which she uses to challenge rivals and allies alike, including Oswald Cobb. Her character embodies a balance of elegance and deadly intent, often masking her true motives behind a calm exterior.

Victor "Vic" Aguilar - The Kid

Victor Aguilar in is a new addition to Gotham's criminal scene, bringing a fresh but intense energy to the storyline. He’s often portrayed as fiercely loyal and highly skilled, particularly in areas that make him a valuable asset in the underworld, like tactical planning and street-level operations. Aguilar’s background and motivations add depth to his character, suggesting a mix of street smarts and raw ambition, with a keen understanding of Gotham’s brutal power dynamics. He aligns himself with powerful figures like The Penguin, but his loyalty may come at a price, hinting at a character who’s as calculating as he is reliable.

Salvatore "Sal" Maroni - The Don

Salvatore Maroni is a classic figure in Gotham’s organized crime network, embodying a fierce, old-school mob boss persona. He’s known for his brutal leadership style and a zero-tolerance policy for betrayal, making him one of the most feared criminals in the city. Maroni held a significant rivalry with Carmine Falcone, competing for control over Gotham’s criminal empire, which inevitably brought him into conflict with newer figures like The Penguin. Despite his ruthless demeanor, Maroni operates with a certain code of honor typical of old mob traditions, balancing savagery with a keen sense of loyalty to his closest associates. His character reflects a deep-seated ambition and an unwillingness to relinquish power, making him a constant, looming threat in Gotham’s underworld.

Julian Rush - The Doctor

Dr. Julian Rush is a sophisticated and enigmatic figure, adding psychological intensity to the narrative. As a doctor, he wields knowledge of the mind as his weapon, using his skills to influence and perhaps manipulate Gotham’s underworld players. Unlike the more overtly violent criminals, Dr. Rush operates with a clinical detachment, preferring to dissect motivations and vulnerabilities to gain leverage. His calm, intellectual approach gives him a quietly menacing aura, suggesting that his intentions may not always be clear, and his allegiance may shift based on his own interests.

Francis "Ma" Cobb - The Mother

Francis Cobblepot, often referred to as "Oz's mother," is portrayed as a significant influence in Oswald Cobblepot's life. She is depicted as a strong-willed and protective figure, deeply devoted to her son. Her nurturing nature and ambitions for Oswald instill in him both a sense of self-worth and a desire for greatness, despite the challenges he faces in Gotham's harsh environment. Francis's character typically embodies the complexity of a mother's love—supportive yet possibly overbearing—reflecting both affection and high expectations for Oswald. Her relationship with him is pivotal, as it not only shapes his ambitions but also his understanding of power and identity within the criminal underbelly of Gotham. The impact of her character resonates throughout the series, influencing Oswald's motivations and actions as he strives to carve out his own legacy.
